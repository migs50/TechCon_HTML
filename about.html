Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a grassroots meetup for local tech enthusiasts. Over the years, it has evolved into an international event attracting thousands of participants from all over the globe.

Our Mission

TechCon's mission is to foster innovation, inspire collaboration, and empower the global tech community. We believe in open knowledge sharing, diversity, and building a future powered by ethical technology.
