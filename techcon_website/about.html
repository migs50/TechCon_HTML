History of TechCon

Founded in 2010, TechCon started as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts in a university basement. The goal was simple: to create a space where developers, designers, and innovators could share their passion for technology and learn from one another. Over the years, it has grown into a premier international conference, attracting thousands of attendees from across the globe, all while staying true to its grassroots origins of fostering community and innovation.

Our Mission

Our mission at TechCon is to inspire and empower the next generation of technology leaders. We aim to provide a platform for showcasing cutting-edge advancements, fostering collaboration, and promoting ethical considerations in the development and deployment of technology. We believe in the power of community to solve the world's most pressing challenges and strive to create an inclusive and welcoming environment for all attendees.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Evelyn Reed

A pioneer in quantum computing, Dr. Reed delivered a groundbreaking keynote in 2022 on the future of quantum cryptography. Her work continues to shape the security landscape of the digital age.

Ben Carter

The lead architect behind the revolutionary 'Nebula' OS, Ben Carter, graced our stage in 2021. He shared invaluable insights into scalable system design and the challenges of building for a billion users.

Aisha Khan

A leading expert in artificial intelligence and ethics, Aisha Khan's 2023 talk on 'AI for Good' challenged the community to think critically about the societal impact of their creations. Her advocacy for responsible AI has been influential worldwide.
